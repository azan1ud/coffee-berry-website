The best things come in small packages.

Coffee Berry is a compact, family-run cafe hidden on Barnsbury Road, Islington. Run by Anne with endless love and care, step inside and you'll find a warm welcome and an aroma you won't forget.

We do things by hand. From freshly made sandwiches and our signature Vietnamese-inspired banh mi baguettes, to meticulously pulled smooth coffee and fresh juices.
